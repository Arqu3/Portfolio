This folder contains a 3D hack-and-slash game inspired by Diablo3 and Path of Exile made with unity, it
showcases click to move mechanics, enemy spawning & basic combat.

The .rar archive contains a runnable .exe file and the source project can be found here:
https://github.com/Arqu3/Project--Hack-n-slash

Instructions:
Click to move
Left click on an enemy when in melee range to attack them
Hold right click to charge an area of effect attack, release button to deal damage to all enemies in front of player
Press tab when in center of map to open the shop, where you can buy health & damage upgrades from the gold
that the enemies drop
Red blocks on the ground heals the player when walked over (like health globes in D3)
